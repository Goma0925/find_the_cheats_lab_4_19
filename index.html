Find the Cheater

This graph aims to find out the cheater in the class by using the correlation between two students' homework scores. The color of the map represents the correlation coefficient. If the color is red for a pair of student, then there is a strong correlation between the scores of those two students, and there is more probability that they are cheating on homework since their scores much over the time in the course. The correspondence of the other colors and the correlation is presented in the legend. We suggest if the color is orange or red, you can start examining the homework and see if they are cheating.
